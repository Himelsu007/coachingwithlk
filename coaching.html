How is life coaching different from therapy?
While therapy often delves into past experiences and emotions, life coaching is future-oriented, focusing on setting and achieving specific goals. Life coaching is action-oriented and aims to help individuals maximize their potential and create a more fulfilling life in the present and future.
What can I expect from a one-on-one coaching session?
Personalization: The coaching sessions are customized to address the unique circumstances and objectives of the individual client. Confidentiality: The nature of one-on-one coaching allows for open and confidential discussions, creating a safe space for the client to explore personal and professional matters.

Individual Attention: The coach dedicates their attention solely to the needs of the client during each session, fostering a deep understanding of the client's situation.

Goal Setting: The coaching process often involves collaboratively setting and working towards specific, measurable, and achievable goals.

Accountability: The coach helps the client stay accountable for their actions, commitments, and progress toward their objectives.

Feedback and Reflection: One-on-One coaching provides a platform for receiving constructive feedback and engaging in reflective conversations to promote personal growth.

This format of coaching is beneficial for individuals seeking highly personalized and targeted support in areas such as career development, personal growth, leadership skills, and life transitions.
