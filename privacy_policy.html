This privacy policy was last updated on April second 2024.

This Privacy Policy outlines the practices of Lara Katerine ("we," "our," or "us") regarding the collection, use, and sharing of personal information when you interact with our website [www.coachingwithlk.com].

Information We Collect

When you browse the Site, we may collect various types of information to enhance your experience. This may include your name, email address, and other personal details voluntarily provided by you through contact forms. Additionally, we use cookies and similar technologies to gather data about your browsing behavior and device information.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized for multiple purposes, including: Communication: We may use your contact information to respond to inquiries, provide updates on services, and facilitate appointments or bookings. Analytics: We analyze website traffic and user behavior patterns to improve the functionality and content of our Site, ensuring a more personalized and engaging experience. Promotional Activities: Your information may be used to promote our coaching services, events, workshops, and other relevant offerings. This may involve sending newsletters, promotional emails, or targeted advertisements.

Data Storage and Protection

Your privacy and security are of utmost importance to us. Personal information collected through the Site is securely stored in an Excel sheet owned and managed by Lara Katerine. We implement industry-standard security measures and protocols to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure.

Sharing of Information

Rest assured, we do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Your data is kept strictly confidential and used solely for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to maintain control over your personal information. Should you wish to have your data deleted or amended, please contact us at admin@coachingwithlk.com. We will promptly address your request in accordance with applicable privacy laws.

Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please don't hesitate to reach out to us at admin@coachingwithlk.com. Your privacy matters to us, and we're committed to ensuring transparency and accountability in how we handle your information.
